В целях минимизации рисков коррупционных проявлений, хищений и мошенничества на предприятиях атомной отрасли генеральным директором госкорпорации «Росатом» С.В. Кириенко утверждена Комплексная программа противодействия коррупции и хищениям в атомной отрасли.

In our effort to prevent illegal behaviour within ROSATOM, we have adopted an anti-corruption and anti-embezzlement programme that provides for an integrated set of industry-specific administrative, economic, legal and human resource measures to minimise the risk of corruption, embezzlement and fraud.

The Programme is based on the best national and international practices, ensuring compliance with the law, intolerance to corruption and prevention of crime.

The Programme introduces efficient controls and feedback channels – instances of corruption can be reported by phone, email or post. Any employee who has come to know any facts of corruption or fraud can report them directly to the Asset Protection Department which is responsible for anti-corruption efforts and which guarantees the confidentiality and an independent review of information that is reported.

The Anti-Corruption Programme has already contributed to building a corruption-free environment within ROSATOM.
